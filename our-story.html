Our Story

At Gharvedtan, we believe that the essence of community is built around trust, tradition, and meaningful human connection. What started as a small idea—to connect people with local, home-based food and property solutions—quickly grew into a movement.

Our journey began with a simple question: How can we empower everyday people to share the warmth of their homes and kitchens with others? In a world dominated by big brands and generic listings, we wanted to create something personal, secure, and rooted in authenticity.

Gharvedtan (which means “Home-based Return”) is more than just a platform. It’s a bridge between:

  • Home cooks offering weekly meals with love and care
  • Sellers listing real estate in their local neighborhoods
  • Buyers and seekers looking for trust, transparency, and personal connection

Today, Gharvedtan connects thousands of users across India, fostering not just business—but relationships, memories, and impact. From a mother preparing tiffins for office-goers to a family selling their ancestral home, every listing tells a story.

We are committed to ethical practices, safe transactions, and innovation that makes real-life impact. With every update, feature, and partnership, we aim to keep our values at the core—trust, community, and empowerment.

Thank you for being part of the Gharvedtan family. We’re just getting started—and this story is only going to grow stronger with you.
